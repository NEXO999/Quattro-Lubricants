About Us

At H&H Construction our core business entails renovations, maintenance, and alterations of private homes and commercial spaces inter-alia offices, retail outlets, and industrial areas. We offer a full spectrum of home improvement services from the kitchen to the bathroom. We provide trusted home improvement solutions.

Our Story

H&H Construction was started in 2010 with 10 years experience and has been actively involved in all aspects of building renovations, alterations, maintenance, and additions specializing in both private homes and commercial spaces.
