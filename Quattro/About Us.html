About Us

At Quattro Lubricants, we are committed to delivering high-performance lubrication solutions that protect your equipment, enhance efficiency, and extend service life. Our products are formulated using virgin base oils and carefully selected additives, ensuring consistent quality, superior stability, and reliable performance under the most demanding conditions. Proudly SABS approved, our lubricants meet rigorous national standards, reflecting our dedication to quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. Whether for automotive, industrial, or engineering applications, Quattro Lubricants provides trusted products designed to keep your machinery running smoothly and efficiently.

Performance Standards

► ENGINE OILS SABS 1361, 1517 & 400A specifications
► HYDRAULIC OILS SABS 1218 specifications
► COMPRESSOR OILS SABS 1218 specifications
► GERMAN SPECIFICATIONS Din 51506, VD-L
► DEGREASING FLUIDS SABS 1216 Type 2, 1344 & 1365 specifications
► GREASE SABS 1851

Product Guarantee

Our products are manufactured using only virgin base stocks (Group II) and exceed the requirements of various SABS
specifications.

The EELQMS Quality Management System annually ensures consistency in quality and complete traceability, from the source of the raw materials, to the finished product.

All products manufactured and supplied are guaranteed (if used in the correct application) and insured.
